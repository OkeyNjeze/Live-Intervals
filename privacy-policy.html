1. Introduction

Welcome to Safe Pulse, operated by Njeze Technologies. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application Safe Pulse. By using Safe Pulse, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Who We Are

Safe Pulse is a family safety and location tracking application designed to help families stay connected and safe. Safe Pulse is developed and operated by Njeze Technologies, an independent software developer based in Nigeria. We are committed to complying with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 and applicable data protection laws.

3. Minimum Age

Safe Pulse is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. By using the app, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will immediately delete their account and all associated data.

4. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Full name — used to identify you within your family group
  • Email address — used for account creation and login
  • Secret code — stored locally on your device only, never sent to our servers

Information We Collect Automatically:

  • Location data (GPS) — collected at intervals you choose while tracking is active
  • Device information — basic device data collected by Firebase for app functionality

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • We do not collect payment card details — all payments are processed securely by Paystack
  • We do not collect contacts, photos, or any data unrelated to app functionality
  • We do not run advertising or analytics tracking

5. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly to:

  • Provide location tracking and family safety features
  • Display your location history to your approved family members
  • Send family invites and manage family groups
  • Process premium subscription payments via Paystack
  • Maintain and improve the app

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is stated above.

6. Location Data

Location data is the core of Safe Pulse. Here is exactly how we handle it:

  • Location is only collected when you actively enable tracking
  • Your location is only visible to family members you have explicitly invited and accepted
  • Location history is automatically deleted after 24 hours for free users and 30 days for premium users
  • You can stop tracking at any time from within the app
  • No one outside your approved family group can see your location

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to any third party. Your data is only shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • Within your family group — location data is visible only to family members you have explicitly invited and accepted
  • Firebase/Google — we use Google Firebase for secure data storage and authentication
  • Paystack — payment processing only. Paystack handles all card data and we never see or store your payment details
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose data if required by Nigerian law or valid legal process

8. Data Retention

  • Free users: Location history is automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • Premium users: Location history is automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • After account deletion: All your data is permanently deleted from our servers within 7 days

9. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data is stored on Google Firebase with strict security rules. No user can access another user's data without explicit permission. Payment data is handled entirely by Paystack. Your disguise secret code is stored only on your device. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Your Rights Under NDPA 2023

  • Right to access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to correction — update your personal information
  • Right to deletion — delete your account from within the app, or email us at safepath@gmail.com and we will process your request within 7 days
  • Right to withdraw consent — stop location tracking at any time

11. Disguise Feature

Safe Pulse includes an optional disguise feature that makes the app appear as another app on your device. The secret code for this feature is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. We have no way to recover your secret code if lost.

12. Third Party Services

  • Google Firebase — data storage, authentication: Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps — map display: Google Privacy Policy
  • Paystack — payment processing: Paystack Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
